Your task is to choose an organization and perform OSINT on its infrastructure and operations.

OSINT on Infrastructure

1) Find any domain names associated with that organization
2) Find any subdomains, if available
3) Find any physical locations that might be associated with the organization or the domains you found

OSINT on Operations

1) Find any hiring requirements
2) Find the latest events of the organization
3) Find the key employees who are recently active on social media about the organizational activities
